White Rice Recipe

Plain is Perfect

Here we dont get caught up with the complicated, we like to stick to the basics. Thats why we will be making Mama's famous plain white rice recipe today.

Here's how to boil long-grain rice on the stovetop! It's an easy, foolproof method that works with white or brown long-grain rice. Great as simple side dish for any weeknight meal.

    What You Will Need

  • 1 cup of long grain white or brown rice
  • Water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    Method

  1. Fill a pot of water and add the rice
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to a simmer
  3. Cook the rice until barely tender
  4. Drain the rice
  5. Return the rice to the pot and let it sit there for a bit
  6. Fluff and stir it

TIPS

If the rice is too wet you messed up and if the rice is too dry you also messed up.
